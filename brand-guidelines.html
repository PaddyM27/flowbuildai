Colour Rules

Never use Amber on Amber. Ensure 4.5:1 contrast ratio minimum on all text colour combinations.

White text on Amber passes WCAG AA at large sizes only. Use Charcoal text for small print on Amber backgrounds.

Off-brand colour use. Do not introduce brand-level usage of third-party colours (blue, purple, teal). Functional colours are the only exception.

Gradients. Amber-to-dark gradients are permitted for hero backgrounds. Avoid light-to-light or rainbow gradients.

Section 05

Voice & Tone

How we speak is as distinctive as how we look. Our clients are trade professionals — plain-speaking people who value results over rhetoric.

05

Direct, not blunt
We say exactly what we mean, with no padding. But we're never rude or dismissive. Clarity is respect.
Not: "Our innovative, cutting-edge platform synergises your lead generation ecosystem."
Specific, not vague
We use numbers, timeframes, and concrete outcomes. "More leads" is lazy. "6 more leads per month by Month 2" is our standard.
Not: "We help businesses grow their online presence."
Knowledgeable, not arrogant
We know trades and we know marketing. We share our expertise generously, but we never talk down to clients or dismiss their experience.
Not: "Most tradespeople have no idea how marketing works."
Warm, not sycophantic
We're human, Irish, and approachable. But we don't flatter. "Great question!" is not a sentence we ever write.
Not: "That's an amazing insight — love that you asked that!"
Copy Examples — Do & Don't
✓ Write Like This
"You're losing around 6 leads a month. We can fix that in 72 hours."
"No lock-in beyond 90 days. If it's not working, you walk."
"We don't do reports. We do results — and we show you exactly what they are."
"Built for Irish trades, not American tech companies."
"Your phone rings while you're on the roof. Our system answers."
✕ Never Write Like This
"Unlock the power of AI-driven synergistic growth solutions."
"We are passionate about helping businesses thrive in the digital age."
"Our innovative approach leverages cutting-edge technology."
"We help you reach your full potential and scale sustainably."
"Take your business to the next level with our transformative platform."

Section 06

Imagery & Photography

Visual choices communicate values before copy is read. Our imagery is honest, specific, and never generic.

06
Photography Principles
🔧
✓ Approved Direction
Real tradespeople at work. Gritty, authentic, high-contrast. Amber colour grade. Natural light, not studio.
📱
✓ Approved Direction
Phones showing results: dashboards, missed call notifications, lead counts. Real outcomes visualised.
👔
✕ Never Use
Stock business people, handshakes, office scenes. This audience sees through generic instantly.
Photography Treatment
Amber Grade + Dark Overlay
Standard Treatment
Apply linear-gradient overlay: amber tint (30% opacity) over dark charcoal (60% opacity at bottom). Creates depth and brand warmth.
Dark Vignette Only
Subtle Treatment
For high-quality photos where the image speaks clearly. Charcoal vignette from bottom only. Used when the subject warrants prominence.
Illustration & Iconography

Icons: Use solid-filled, single-weight icons from a consistent set (Phosphor or Heroicons Solid). Never outline icons alongside solid ones. Size: 16px, 20px, 24px, 32px only.

Illustration: Avoid decorative illustration. If used, it must be minimal, geometric, and in brand colours only. No isometric 3D, no cartoon characters, no abstract swirls.
